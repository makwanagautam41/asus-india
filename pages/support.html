Frequently Asked Questions

To register your ASUS product, visit our official website and navigate to the product registration page. You'll need your product's serial number, which can be found on the bottom of your device or in the original packaging. Registration helps us provide you with better support and keeps you informed about important updates.
ASUS warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. This includes components like the motherboard, display, keyboard, and internal hardware. The standard warranty period is typically 1-2 years depending on your product. Accidental damage, liquid damage, and unauthorized modifications are not covered.
You can update your drivers through the ASUS Support website by entering your model number, or use the MyASUS app which automatically detects your system and recommends available updates. We recommend keeping your drivers up to date for optimal performance and security.
First, ensure your laptop is properly charged by connecting it to the power adapter for at least 30 minutes. Try a hard reset by holding the power button for 40 seconds. If the issue persists, disconnect all external devices and try again. If these steps don't work, please contact our support team for further assistance.
To claim warranty service, contact our support center with your product serial number and proof of purchase. Our team will verify your warranty status and guide you through the repair process. Depending on your location, we offer mail-in service, walk-in service centers, or on-site support for eligible products.
Upgrade options vary by model. Many ASUS laptops allow RAM and storage upgrades, but some ultraportable models have soldered components. Check your product manual or contact support to confirm upgrade options for your specific model. Note that unauthorized modifications may void your warranty.

Still Need Help?

Our support team is here to assist you. Whether you have a technical question, need warranty service, or want to learn more about your ASUS product, we're ready to help.

You can reach us through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM IST.
