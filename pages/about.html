About ASUS

In Search of Incredible - Our commitment to innovation and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 1989, ASUS has grown to become one of the world's leading technology companies. Our name comes from Pegasus, the winged horse in Greek mythology, representing the spirit of innovation and the pursuit of perfection.

From our humble beginnings as a motherboard manufacturer, we've expanded to create a diverse range of products including laptops, desktops, graphics cards, monitors, and networking equipment. Today, ASUS products are used by millions of people worldwide.

Our Mission

"In Search of Incredible" is more than just our tagline—it's our philosophy. We believe in pushing the boundaries of what's possible, creating products that inspire and empower our customers to achieve their goals.

Whether you're a gamer seeking the ultimate performance, a creative professional demanding color accuracy, a business executive requiring reliability, or a student looking for value, ASUS has a solution designed specifically for you.

Innovation & Quality

At ASUS, quality is paramount. Every product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards. We invest heavily in research and development, holding thousands of patents and winning numerous industry awards.

Our commitment to innovation has led to groundbreaking products like the ROG gaming series, ZenBook ultraportables, and ProArt creator laptops. We continue to pioneer new technologies that shape the future of computing.

Sustainability

We recognize our responsibility to the environment and are committed to sustainable practices. From energy-efficient products to eco-friendly packaging, we're working to reduce our environmental impact while delivering exceptional technology.

Our goal is to create products that not only perform exceptionally but also contribute to a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Join Our Journey

Whether you're looking for your next laptop, seeking career opportunities, or interested in partnering with us, we invite you to be part of the ASUS story. Together, we can continue to push the boundaries of innovation.
